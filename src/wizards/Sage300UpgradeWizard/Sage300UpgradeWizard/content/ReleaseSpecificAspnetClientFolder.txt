Reporting now requires the use of components from the 'aspnet_client' folder.

Please locate this folder on your system and copy it, as well as all of it's 
content to the root of your custom solutions 'Web' project.

A typical location on your system where this folder can be found is as 
follows:

C:\inetpub\wwwroot\aspnet_client\system_web\4.0.30319\

It's not necessary to copy the content from all versions of Microsoft.NET
Only version 4.0.30319 is necessary.
